Privacy Policy

What we offer

Effective Date: August 13, 2024

Welcome to SpamShield, a Smart Email Classifier designed to help users identify and manage spam, phishing, and other unwanted emails. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our application. When you use SpamShield, we may collect personal information such as your email address, and google app password. Our application processes email content, including subject lines, body text, attachments, and metadata, to classify and filter spam, phishing, and other types of emails.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. We may share your data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our application, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep your information confidential.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Effective Date" will be updated accordingly. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates.
